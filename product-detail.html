Is this reel suitable for saltwater use?

Yes! The Carbon X 3000 features IPX7 waterproof construction and sealed bearings, making it excellent for saltwater fishing. We recommend rinsing with fresh water after each saltwater session for optimal longevity.

What pound test line should I use?

The 3000 size is optimal for 8-12lb monofilament or 15-30lb braided line. This makes it perfect for bass, walleye, and medium-sized saltwater species.

Is the handle reversible for left-hand retrieve?

Yes, the Carbon X 3000 features a reversible handle that can be easily switched for left or right-hand retrieve without any tools.

What does the lifetime warranty cover?

Our lifetime warranty covers all manufacturing defects and material failures. Normal wear items like drag washers and bearings are covered for 3 years. Simply contact our support team and we'll send you a prepaid shipping label.
