Our Story

BUILT BY ANGLERS.
FOR ANGLERS.

We craft premium fishing tackle that performs when it matters most — when you're face-to-face with the catch of a lifetime.

Our Mission

ENGINEERING
EXCELLENCE

Since 2008, APEX Tackle has been at the forefront of fishing tackle innovation. What started in a small garage in San Diego has grown into a global brand trusted by professional anglers worldwide.

Our mission is simple: create the most advanced, reliable, and high-performing fishing tackle on the market. We achieve this through relentless research, Japanese precision engineering, and real-world testing with our pro staff in some of the most demanding fishing conditions on earth.

Our Journey

THE APEX STORY

2008

The Beginning

Founded in San Diego by lifelong angler Michael Chen. First product: the original APEX Carbon Rod, hand-built in limited quantities.

2012

Reel Revolution

Launch of the Carbon X series reels featuring our proprietary MagSeal technology. Won "Best New Reel" at ICAST.

2016

Going Global

Expanded to international markets with distribution across North America, Europe, Japan, and Australia.

2020

Pro Team Dominance

APEX Pro Staff wins three major tournament championships. Total tournament winnings exceed $2 million.

2024

Next Generation

Introduction of AI-assisted rod design and sustainable manufacturing practices. Carbon neutral by 2025.
